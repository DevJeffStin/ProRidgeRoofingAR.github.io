Jefferson Stinson

Email: Jefferson@Stinson.Dev

Location: Central Arkansas

Occupation: Systems Specialist

Employer: United States District Court for the Eastern District of Arkansas

Jefferson Stinson is a developer currently working at the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Arkansas, with years of experience and a Bachelors degree in computer science. He has worked on many projects, including three award-winning websites and other large-scale web software projects while at DNT Media. Additionally, he has contributed to large web systems within the courts. His preferred languages are PHP, HTML, JavaScript, and MySQL, but he also has experience in C#, C++, PERL, Python, and Visual Basic. He is proficient in server maintenance for both Windows and Linux systems, handling both hardware and software.

Besides development, Jefferson is an avid PC gamer, LEGO enthusiast, photographer, gun collector, and trail rider. He plays a variety of games, including Kerbal Space Program, Counter-Strike 2, Escape From Tarkov, Fallout, and the occasional Fortnite. As a LEGO enthusiast, he primarily builds Star Wars and Harry Potter sets, but he also enjoys constructing more intricate space and car models. Trail riding with his dad is one of his favorite hobbies; Arkansas has many beautiful locations for riding, and taking an old Jeep through the trails is always enjoyable.

Born in Hawaii to a military family, Jefferson's father served in the US Army. During his younger years, he moved around to Louisiana and Georgia, finally settling in the small town of Atkins, Arkansas, where his mother's family is from. He graduated high school in 2017 in a class of 65 students. Jefferson then attended Arkansas Tech University to pursue his bachelor's degree in computer science while working at Chick-fil-A.
